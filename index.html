About Us

A Legacy of Healing & Compassionate Care

Sivapreethi Hospital is a trusted multi-specialty medical centre in Thanjavur, dedicated to providing accessible and high-quality healthcare to every patient who walks through our doors.

  • Expert team of specialist doctors across multiple disciplines
  • Modern diagnostic and treatment facilities
  • Dedicated Diabetic Patient's Welfare Trust for affordable care
  • Patient Rights & Responsibilities charter for transparent treatment
  • Comfortable hospital stay facilities for admitted patients
  • 24/7 emergency care and ambulance support
Our Services

Comprehensive Medical Departments

From routine check-ups to complex treatments, our specialists cover a wide range of medical disciplines to serve your every healthcare need.

🩺

General Health

🛡️

Immunizations

Psychiatry

Emergency Care

Round-the-clock emergency medical services with a dedicated team ready to handle critical and acute conditions.
